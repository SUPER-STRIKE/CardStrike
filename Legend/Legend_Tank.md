If this unit plays cards with the **same total Mana cost for 4 consecutive turns**:

- Restore **all Mana spent during those 4 turns**
- Deal damage equal to **2× the restored Mana**
- This damage is **distributed evenly among all other players**

## `☠️ Death Knight – Undying`

Whenever an enemy hero dies, summon a **Bone Knight**.


### 🦴 Bone Knight

- Base HP equals **30% of the fallen hero’s Max HP**
- Each additional Bone Knight summoned increases this scaling by **+30%**
- Maximum scaling: **150% of the fallen hero’s Max HP**

### 🛡️ Effect

- Bone Knights **redirect all damage taken by the Death Knight to themselves**
- Exception: **area/splash damage is not redirected**

## `🧘 Monk – Ascetic`

Whenever any player takes damage:

- Heal this unit for **5 HP**
- Draw **1 card**

### ✋ Card Restriction

This unit can only play cards if their hand size is at least:  
**(N + 6)**, where N = number of players currently alive in the lobby.

### ✨ Enlightenment Effect

Each time this unit deals damage with a card:

- That damage is **copied once**
- The copy is dealt to a **random different enemy target**
- This also applies to **splash/area damage**

Additionally:

- Both affected enemies **cannot target the Monk for 1 turn**
